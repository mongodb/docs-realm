==============
Realm (Legacy)
==============

{+service+} uses {+client-database+} version 10 and above. The
documentation for earlier versions of {+client-database+} can be found
on at `Realm documentation (legacy)
<https://www.mongodb.com/docs/realm-legacy/docs/>`_.
