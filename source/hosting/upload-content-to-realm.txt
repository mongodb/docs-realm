Overview
--------

You can upload content to Realm from the :guilabel:`Hosting` screen of
the Realm UI or by :doc:`importing
</deploy/deploy-changes-with-realm-cli>` an application directory that
includes the files. Select the tab below that corresponds to the method
you want to use.
