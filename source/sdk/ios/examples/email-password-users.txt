===========================
Manage Email/Password Users
===========================

.. default-domain:: mongodb

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 2
   :class: singlecol

<email-password-authentication>` in your {+app+}, you can register a new
account, confirm an email address, and reset a user's password from
client code.
