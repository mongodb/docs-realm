:kotlin-sdk:`RealmConfiguration <{+kotlin-local-prefix+}io.realm/-realm-configuration/index.html>` with

and pass the resulting :file:`RealmConfiguration` to

Close a Realm
-------------

You can close a realm with :kotlin-sdk:`realm.close() <io.realm/-realm/close.html?>`.
:file:`close()` blocks until all write transactions to the realm finish.
