## Open a Realm

To open a realm, create a
[`RealmConfiguration`](https://docs.mongodb.com/realm-sdks/kotlin/latest/library-base/-realm%20-kotlin%20-multiplatform%20-s-d-k/io.realm/-realm-configuration/index.html) with
[`RealmConfiguration.Builder`](https://docs.mongodb.com/realm-sdks/kotlin/latest/library-base/-realm%20-kotlin%20-multiplatform%20-s-d-k/io.realm/-realm-configuration/-builder/index.html)
and pass the resulting `RealmConfiguration` to
[`Realm.open()`](https://docs.mongodb.com/realm-sdks/kotlin/latest/library-base/-realm%20-kotlin%20-multiplatform%20-s-d-k/io.realm/-realm/-companion/open.html):

<CodeBlock language="kotlin">{Open}</CodeBlock>

## Close a Realm

You can close a realm with [`realm.close()`](https://docs.mongodb.com/realm-sdks/kotlin/latest/io.realm/-realm/close.html?).
The `close()` method blocks until all write transactions on the
realm have completed.
